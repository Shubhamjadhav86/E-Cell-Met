Executive Summary

MET’S Institute of Technology's E-Cell successfully secured All India Rank 05 (AIR-05) at the National Entrepreneurship Challenge, hosted during the E-Summit ‘24 at IIT Bombay. This prestigious achievement underscores the institute’s emerging status as a hub for innovation and leadership.

Departmental Strategy

The success was driven by a structured corporate model consisting of four specialized departments:

  • Operations: Managed internal timelines and 100% accurate documentation.
  • Events: Conducted 20+ on-campus workshops and ideation sessions.
  • Design: Created the professional NEC Portfolio and visual brand identity.
  • Marketing: Maximized the digital footprint and official Instagram presence.

Key Achievement Highlights

  • Official recognition as a "Top 5 Entrepreneurial Campus" in India.
  • Successful execution of the "MET’S Model of Collaborative Excellence."
  • Represented by a 13-member student delegation at the IIT Bombay Grand Finale.
  • Significant boost in institutional brand equity among industry leaders.
