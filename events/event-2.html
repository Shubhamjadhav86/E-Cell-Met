Eureka! 2024: Road to Enterprise

ZONAL BUSINESS MODEL COMPETITION • IIT BOMBAY COLLABORATION
+0

Executive Summary

On September 30, 2024, the Entrepreneurship Cell (E-Cell) of MET Institute of Technology successfully organized and hosted "Eureka! 2024 - Road to Enterprise." This event served as a vital Zonal-level entry point for Asia’s largest business model competition in prestigious collaboration with IIT Bombay.

The initiative aimed to identify disruptive startup ideas, offering a combined incentive pool exceeding ₹2 Crores and a structured pathway to national recognition.

Strategic Objectives

  • Innovation Scouting: Funneling high-potential business models from Engineering, Management, and Pharmacy.
  • Ecosystem Synergy: Bridging the gap between regional Tier-2 city innovators and the global startup ecosystem.
  • Global Competitiveness: Preparing students for international stages with networking trips to the USA and Dubai.

The Partnership: MET & IIT Bombay

IIT Bombay: Provided the standardized competition framework, evaluation rubrics, and national-level platform.

MET E-Cell: Acted as the Zonal Executive body, managing logistics, outreach, and the execution of preliminary pitching rounds.

The Pitching Protocol

  • 5-Minute Pitch: Teams presented Problem Statements, Technical Solutions, Revenue Models, and Scalability.
  • 2-Minute Q&A: A specialized panel of judges conducted rapid-fire questioning to test market research depth.

Judging Criteria (100-Point Scale)

  • Innovation (25%): Uniqueness of the solution.
  • Market Potential (25%): Target market size and sustainability.
  • Feasibility (25%): Technical ability to deliver the product.
  • Defense (25%): Ability to handle critical feedback.

Rewards & Impact

Top-performing teams received "Fast Track" entry into the IIT Bombay E-Summit, access to coworking spaces, legal mentorship, and institutional incubation.

  • Regional Validation: 3 teams officially recognized as Top Performers.
  • Institutional Prestige: Cemented MET's role as a leader in regional entrepreneurship.
