Executive Summary

On November 26, 2025, the Entrepreneurship Cell (E-Cell) organized the "MET Illuminate Workshop," a high-impact seminar designed to catalyze the entrepreneurial spirit. The workshop moved students away from conventional employment mindsets toward independent venture creation.

Keynote: The 48-Hour Startup Framework

Delivered by Leadership Coach Mr. Amiit Dabke, the session transitioned from leadership psychology to a rapid-execution business framework.

  • The Leadership Mindset: Strategies for reframing failure as "iterative learning" and mastering self-leadership.
  • Value Proposition: Identifying specific market pain points.
  • Lean Operations: Starting small to minimize financial risk and building a Minimum Viable Product (MVP).

The "Illuminate" Starter Kits

To ensure lasting impact, E-Cell distributed specialized Starter Kits containing:

  • The Illuminate Guidebook: A manual containing the frameworks discussed.
  • Planning Tools: Checklists and templates for initial business goal setting.
  • Branded Merchandise: High-quality gear to maintain community identity.
