The Tri-Phase Challenge

To reach the podium, the team navigated three specialized competitive tracks:

1. Idea Ignite: Presentation of a data-driven business model focused on market viability and technical feasibility.
2. Frame by Frame: Operational analysis round requiring real-time problem solving and identification of business bottlenecks.
3. Brand Wars: A high-octane marketing simulation managing reputation and market share against aggressive competitors.
