IPL Mock Auction 2.0

STRATEGIC SIMULATION • FINANCIAL ACUMEN
+0

Executive Summary

On November 22, 2025, the E-Cell executed the IPL Mock Auction 2.0. This simulation was designed to test students' financial acumen and real-time strategic agility. Teams managed virtual purses to acquire high-profile assets, bridging the gap between sports and Resource Allocation.

Version 2.0 Evolution: Mandated category balancing (Batsmen/Bowlers/All-rounders) and strategic inflation simulation were introduced to mimic real-world market volatility.

Core Pedagogical Objectives

Dynamic Budgeting

Practicing the "Envelope Method" to compartmentalize funds across different asset classes.

Market Psychology

Navigating the "Bandwagon Effect" and maintaining objectivity during high-tempo bidding wars.

Technical & Data Integration

The event utilized a custom digital dashboard to ensure competitive accuracy:

  • Real-time Purse Depletion: Instant updates on team spending capacity.
  • Squad Composition Tracking: Automated verification of roster requirements.
  • Price Trend Analysis: Tracking value fluctuations from Marquee to Uncapped talent.

Results & Impact

The event concluded with a "Squad Review" where rosters were evaluated for balance and efficiency. The winners were awarded ₹1000 and formal recognition for strategic maneuvers like the "Steal of the Day."
