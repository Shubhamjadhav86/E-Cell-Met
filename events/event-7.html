Executive Summary

On November 29, 2024, MET E-Cell, in collaboration with Connecting Beings Events, hosted "Yamaha’s Gear On & Throttle." This experiential event immersed students in professional automotive culture through Virtual Reality simulations and technical riding demonstrations.

Pillar Focus: The event bridged the gap between global automotive engineering and the student community, focusing on Yamaha’s "Blue Core" technology and high-scale corporate partnership management.

Technical Simulations & Skill Challenges

Final Lap VR

High-fidelity simulators testing lean angles and braking points.

Slow-Balance

Testing clutch control and body posture on narrow planks.

Safety & Protective Gear Modules

A dedicated "Rider Safety" zone featured professional instructors who demonstrated:

  • Helmet Standards: Comparing DOT, ECE, and ISI impact-resistant certifications.
  • CE-Rated Armor: Showcasing abrasion-resistant jackets and joint protection technology.
  • Ergonomics: Proper seating techniques to reduce fatigue and increase response time.

Impact Analysis

The event achieved record-breaking footfall, fostering a "leaderboard culture" through competitive gaming. Beyond marketing, it successfully advocated for safety among a high-risk demographic, proving E-Cell's versatility in handling massive outdoor corporate activations.
