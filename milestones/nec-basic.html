About Western Entrepreneurship Challenge (WEC)

The Western Entrepreneurship Challenge is a prestigious two-month entrepreneurship championship hosted by E-Cell KITCOEK (Kolhapur Institute of Technology’s College of Engineering). It is designed to strengthen E-Cells across western India and empower student innovators.

  • Structured Growth: Team building, ideation, execution, and evaluation.
  • Mentorship: Guidance from experienced entrepreneurs and industry leaders.
  • Real-World Simulation: Challenges testing leadership and resilience.
  • Networking: Connections with peers, mentors, and startup communities.
  • Impact: A platform to showcase student-led innovation.
